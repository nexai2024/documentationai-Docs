---
title: No Scam Alerts Received
description: What to do if you are not getting NexAI alerts.
---

## Symptoms

- Not receiving scam alert notifications
- Dashboard shows zero alerts detected

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Notification Settings">
    Ensure alerts are enabled for your account in NexAI Settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Contact Info">
    Make sure your email or phone number is correct and verified.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Alert">
    Send a test message using the NexAI dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
