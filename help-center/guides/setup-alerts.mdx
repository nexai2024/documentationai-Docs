## Overview

Configure real-time scam detection alerts in NexAI.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into Dashboard">
    Go to `https://dashboard.nexai.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Alerts">
    Click on "Alerts" in the main sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Notification Method">
    Choose email, SMS, or Slack for alert delivery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Risk Threshold">
    Adjust how sensitive NexAI is to potential scams.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Settings">
    Click "Save" to activate your alerts.
  </Step>
</Steps>
